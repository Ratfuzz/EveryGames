69th Hunger Games

Arena

The arena was a burning desert made up of sand, boulders, and scruffy bushes.

It is likely the only water sources were the cornicopia and sponsor gifts.

For the 25th hunger games onward tributes were put in an enclosed arena.

Victor

There are three people that can be allocated to the 66th, 68th, and 69th hunger games, therefore one of them MUST have won these games: Dolly Kenworthy (D10, Dead), John Doe (D10, Dead) or John Doe (D6, Dead)

It is very unlikely these games were won by the Male Morphling from District 6 (Labeled John Doe) due to hiding the whole games not being plausible in a desert and District 10 tributes being adept to high temperatures.
